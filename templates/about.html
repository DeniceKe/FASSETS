About The System

FASSETS helps the Faculty of Science track assets from registration to maintenance and reporting.

The proposal describes a centralized web-based platform for managing faculty assets securely across departments, with accountability, structured records, and role-based access control.

Centralized asset registry Role-based access Assignment and return tracking

What Users Get

A secure operational workspace for the whole asset lifecycle

Users can work within their assigned scope while administrators and leadership maintain oversight of departments, inventory, maintenance activity, and reporting.

User Management

Role-based access

Supports proposal roles such as Admin, Dean, COD, Lecturer, and Lab Technician, with department-aware access to data and operations.

Asset Management

Register and classify assets

Capture assets in a centralized database, attach categories and locations, manage status, and maintain consistent identification.

Assignment Workflow

Track issue and return

Allocate assets to users or labs, monitor return expectations, and prevent double assignment of unavailable items.

Maintenance

Log service history

Record preventive and corrective maintenance, track status, assign technicians, and keep resolution history in one place.

Reporting

Support decision-making

Generate structured views of asset distribution, assignment activity, and maintenance history for better oversight.

Accountability

Reduce loss and confusion

By replacing scattered records with a shared platform, the system improves transparency, accuracy, and administrative efficiency.
